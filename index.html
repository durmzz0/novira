Scan the Market

We continuously monitor the top 200 highest-volume coins on Binance, across multiple timeframes — from 15-minute scalps to weekly macro trends.

2

Run 36 Indicators

Every coin is evaluated through a mathematical coalition of 36 distinct technical indicators, synchronized across multiple timeframe matrices — no single indicator decides the outcome alone.

3

The combined result is converted into an emotionless score between 51 and 100. Only the strongest, most consistent setups are surfaced as signals.

Every signal is logged the moment it's generated — win, loss, or pending — and tracked live in the Stats tab. Nothing is filtered or adjusted after the fact.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Novira Trade free to use?

Yes. The platform is completely free to use until 01/01/2027, giving everyone the chance to experience its full capabilities.

Does Novira Trade give investment advice?

No. Novira Trade provides data-driven analysis scores and signals for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial or investment advice. All final decisions remain solely with the user — see our Risk Disclaimer for details.

Which devices and Android versions are supported?

Novira Trade works on any Android device (Samsung, Xiaomi, Huawei, Oppo, and others). For the smoothest experience, we recommend Android 11 or higher, battery optimization disabled for the app, and a non-rooted device.

The app isn't loading data / getting stuck. What should I do?

This is most often related to device-specific security layers in Turkey affecting the connection to our data source. Connecting through a VPN typically resolves this immediately. Also check that battery optimization is disabled for the app and that you're on Android 11+. We are actively working on a permanent fix.

Where does the market data come from?

All market data is sourced from Binance's public API. We do not transmit or store your exchange credentials — the app only reads public market data.

What does the "Win Rate" on the Stats page mean?

It reflects the percentage of closed signals that hit their take-profit target versus stop-loss, based on all signals logged so far. As noted in our transparency note, current numbers (120 signals) are still an early sample — the true long-term rate will become clearer at 1,000–5,000 logged signals.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. We only collect what's needed for account authentication (email, name, date of birth, phone, and a device ID) — never payment credentials or trading account access. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I request my data to be deleted?

Yes. Contact us at contact.novirasoft@gmail.com and we'll process your deletion request within 30 days.

Will there be an iOS or Play Store version?

We'd like to expand to more platforms in the future, but there is no confirmed timeline yet. Follow our Instagram for updates.

Hangi cihazlar ve Android sürümleri destekleniyor?

Novira Trade her Android cihazda çalışır (Samsung, Xiaomi, Huawei, Oppo ve diğerleri). En iyi deneyim için Android 11 veya üzeri, uygulama için pil optimizasyonunun kapalı olması ve cihazın rootlu olmaması önerilir.

Uygulama veri çekmiyor / donuyor. Ne yapmalıyım?

Bu durum genellikle Türkiye'deki bazı cihazlara özgü güvenlik katmanlarının veri kaynağımıza bağlantıyı etkilemesinden kaynaklanır. Bir VPN üzerinden bağlanmak genellikle bu sorunu anında çözer. Ayrıca uygulama için pil optimizasyonunun kapalı olduğundan ve Android 11+ kullandığınızdan emin olun. Bu konunun kalıcı çözümü için aktif olarak çalışıyoruz.

Piyasa verileri nereden geliyor?

Tüm piyasa verileri Binance'in herkese açık API'sinden alınmaktadır. Borsa hesap bilgilerinizi asla iletmiyor veya saklamıyoruz — uygulama sadece herkese açık piyasa verilerini okur.

Stats sayfasındaki "Win Rate" ne anlama geliyor?

Şu ana kadar kayıt altına alınan tüm sinyaller içinde, kâr al hedefine ulaşan sinyallerin zarar durdur seviyesine göre oranını gösterir. Şeffaflık notumuzda belirttiğimiz gibi, şu anki veriler (120 sinyal) hâlâ ilk örneklemdir — gerçek uzun vadeli oran 1.000-5.000 kayıtlı sinyalde daha netleşecektir.

Kişisel verilerim güvende mi?

Verilerimin silinmesini talep edebilir miyim?
